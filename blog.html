Quick Wins

Sustainability Tips

Unplug Devices

Vampire power drains energy. Unplug electronics when fully charged or not in use.

BYO Bags

Keep reusable canvas bags in your car or backpack so you never forget them.

Cold Water Wash

Wash clothes in cold water. It saves energy and helps your clothes last much longer.

Meatless Mondays

Skipping meat just one day a week significantly reduces your personal carbon footprint.
