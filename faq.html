General & Products

Orders & Shipping

Domestic orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days. Because we use carbon-neutral shipping methods, sometimes transit can take an extra day, but it's worth it for the planet!
Currently, we ship within the United States, Canada, and select countries in Europe. We are actively working on expanding our reach globally while maintaining a low carbon footprint.
We offer a 30-day "no questions asked" return policy on unused items. Due to hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns on opened personal care items like toothbrushes or soaps. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

Sustainability & Impact

We partner with certified environmental agencies to calculate the exact emissions of our operations and shipping. We then purchase verified carbon offsets that fund reforestation and clean energy projects worldwide.

Product Care Guides

Bamboo Care

Never leave bamboo sitting in water. Hand wash with mild soap and let it air dry completely. Oil monthly to prevent cracking.

Beeswax Wraps

Wash only in COOL water. Hot water will melt the wax. Avoid using with raw meat. Store in a cool, dry place.
