Transparency First

The Crafting Process

See exactly how this tee goes from a bamboo shoot to your wardrobe.

1. Regenerative Harvesting

The bamboo for this tee is harvested from an FSC-certified forest in Indonesia. Bamboo regenerates from its own roots, requiring zero replanting and preventing topsoil erosion.

2. Solar-Powered Knitting

The raw fibers are spun into ultra-soft yarn and knitted in a small, family-owned factory powered entirely by rooftop solar panels. No toxic chemicals are used in the softening process.

3. Plastic-Free Fulfillment

Your tee is folded, wrapped in unbleached recycled tissue paper, and shipped in a 100% compostable cornstarch mailer. The carbon for shipping is automatically offset.

Product Care

How To Make It Last

Cold Wash Only

Machine wash cold with like colors. This saves energy and protects the natural fibers.

Air Dry

Skip the dryer entirely. Hang or lay flat to dry to prevent shrinking and extend the garment's life.

No Bleach

Avoid harsh chemicals and bleach to keep the natural, plant-based dyes perfectly vibrant.
